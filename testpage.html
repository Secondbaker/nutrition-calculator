Mobile Muscle

THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN FLEXIBILITY AND MOBILITY.

Flexibility is your ability to reach positions and ranges of motion, whereas mobility is your ability to control positions and ranges of motion. One word makes a big difference.

ABOUT CONTROLLED ARTICULAR ROTATIONS (CARs).

CARs are best described as 3-D joint rotations, done with an emphasis on moving each joint through as large of a range of motion as possible, while avoiding compensations and “noise.”

ABOUT THE SEQUENCE OF DOOM (SoD).

The Sequence of Doom is a mixture of relaxed stretching, isometric stretching, and end range strength exercises done to bridge the gap between flexibility and mobility.
